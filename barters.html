01 —

the brief

Barter's Travelnet — founded in 1865, the longest-established travel agency in Ireland — needed to look the way it actually felt: warm, community-led, and full of stories. The existing identity was tired and inconsistent across signage, social, print and the website.

The remit grew quickly: a new logo and visual identity, a fresh website, social and content templates that the in-house team could run with, and — somewhere along the way — a mascot named Bartie and a travel podcast.

02 —

what I did

Brand identity

New logo system, "Endless Discoveries" lockup, colour palette, type, and a flexible visual language for print, signage and social.

Bartie the mascot

A walking "B" with a location pin for a head — the agency's friendly avatar. Worked across cupcakes, totes, window vinyls, and a podcast cover.

Website redesign

Designed and helped build the new bartertravelnet.com — clearer search, better holiday merchandising, and a brand-led look.

Bartie's Wanderlogue

Co-created a travel podcast with the in-house team. I co-host, produce and edit — with cover art that extends the new brand world.

the receipts

03 —

the outcome

"Republic of Ireland's Top Agency at the TTG Top 50 Travel Agencies, 2023."

The judges cited the rebrand and the launch of Bartie's Wanderlogue as standout work. Beyond the trophy, the new identity scaled cleanly across shopfronts in Cork, the website, podcast platforms, gift cards and merch — giving the team a single visual language to run with.
